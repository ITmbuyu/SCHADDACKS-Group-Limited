About us

SHADDRACKS GROUPS LIMITED DRC is a company based on the DNA of diversity as its core value. As the Democratic Republic of Congo has started a new chapter in democratic values, and human rights protection, we want to help build infrastructure that will bring more investors to the country. We will be a part of the ongoing efforts to establish a new democracy, which brings with it economic growth and political stability. The strategic objective of SCHADDRACKS Group Limited DRC is to become a well-established and well respected distributor / supplier, with the chance to build a legacy that is built on trust, integrity, and respect for others. Looking forward to conquering the DRC market.

Our History

SCHADDRACKS LOGISTICS & REPAIRS was founded in DURBAN, KWAZULU NATAL PROVINCE after noticing the power of diversification and business growth opportunities there.

OUR MISSION

We aim to provide quality services to our customers and to maintain strong relationships with them. Our team is always open to working with honest partners in the Democratic Republic of Congo as opportunities arise. Unity is our guiding principle. Having it strengthens us and helps us achieve more.
